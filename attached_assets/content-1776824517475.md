## Microcredentials

Validate your hands-on cloud computing skills through real-time assessment in a provisioned AWS environment. Microcredentials complement AWS Certifications by demonstrating your practical implementation abilities.

**Why earn both Certifications and Microcredentials?**

- Certifications validate your broad technical knowledge and understanding
- Microcredentials verify your ability to identify, troubleshoot, and implement AWS solutions in AWS environments
- Together, they provide comprehensive validation of both your knowledge and practical abilities

Available through AWS Skill Builder Individual and Team subscriptions.

[Learn more about Microcredentials](https://skillbuilder.aws/category/type/microcredentials)
